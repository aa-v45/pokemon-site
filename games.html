Games & Trading Card Game Overview

Pokémon includes both video games and a trading card game, and many fans enjoy both sides of the franchise. This page introduces the main ideas of each and points you to the Strategies and Collectibles pages for more detail.

Video Games

In the mainline games, you travel through regions, collect Pokémon, and challenge Gym Leaders or similar trials. Battles are turn-based, which gives you time to think about your moves and type matchups.

  • Catch, train, and evolve Pokémon to build a strong team.
  • Explore towns, routes, caves, and special areas.
  • Battle NPCs and friends locally or online.

For practical tips on building teams and winning battles, visit the Strategies page.

Trading Card Game (TCG)

The Pokémon TCG lets you battle using decks of cards that represent Pokémon, moves, items, and more. Each card has its own stats and effects, and players build decks to match their personal style.

  • Decks typically include Pokémon, Energy, Trainer, and Item cards.
  • Players take turns playing cards and attacking.
  • Competitive play exists both in-person and online.

To learn more about card rarity and collecting, visit the Collectibles page.
