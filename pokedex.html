Pokédex Highlights

This Pokédex page shows a small sample of well-known Pokémon. Each profile includes type information, basic strengths and weaknesses, and a short description to help new fans understand what makes each Pokémon special.

Pikachu

Type: Electric

  • Strong against: Water, Flying
  • Weak to: Ground

Pikachu is the mascot of the Pokémon franchise. It is quick, friendly, and often introduced as a new player’s first Electric-type partner.

Charizard

Type: Fire/Flying

  • Strong against: Grass, Bug, Ice
  • Weak to: Rock, Water, Electric

Charizard is a fan favorite thanks to its powerful Fire-type attacks and dragon-like appearance. It has appeared in many games, shows, and cards.

Bulbasaur

Type: Grass/Poison

  • Strong against: Water, Ground, Rock
  • Weak to: Fire, Flying, Ice

Bulbasaur is known for its balance and support moves. It is often recommended as a starter for players who enjoy a slower, steady style.
