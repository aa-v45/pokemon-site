Starter Pokémon Basics

Starter Pokémon are usually the first partners you receive in a region. They are often Grass, Fire, or Water types, and your choice can shape how your early battles feel. This page gives a simple breakdown so new Trainers can pick a starter that feels right for them.

Grass Starters

Grass starters often have solid defenses and learn moves that slowly wear down an opponent, such as status or healing moves.

  • Strong against Water and Ground types.
  • Can heal and boost themselves in battle.
  • Need to watch out for Fire and Flying types.

Fire Starters

Fire starters are usually aggressive and fast. Their strong Fire-type moves can quickly knock out early-game Grass and Bug opponents.

  • Good at dealing damage quickly.
  • Strong against Grass, Bug, and Ice types.
  • Often have lower defenses.

Water Starters

Water starters are flexible and reliable. They have useful moves and fewer weaknesses, which makes them easy to use in many battles.

  • Strong against Fire, Rock, and Ground.
  • Usually only weak to Grass and Electric.
  • Often learn both physical and special attacks.
